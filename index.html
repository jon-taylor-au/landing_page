Built From Law, Then Code

GovStream is a wholly Australian technology company delivering risk-assured, secure and scalable technology solutions for the Australian Government. GovStream brings together the expertise and resources of premium software development and AI technology firm Adaca, and Sparke Helmore—a leading provider of legal services to the Commonwealth.

Built from law, then code, GovStream’s solutions are designed by a multidisciplinary team involving government law experts with deep knowledge of Commonwealth laws, parameters and operations. Developed specifically for the Australian Government’s needs and frameworks and aligned with its digital, AI and cybersecurity policies, our solutions boost agency efficiency and effectiveness by streamlining workflows, supporting governance, enhancing decision-making and processes, and ensuring legal and operational integrity.
